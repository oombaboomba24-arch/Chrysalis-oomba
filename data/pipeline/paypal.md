# PayPal

## Business Overview
PayPal is a global payments and fintech platform with 430M+ active accounts (consumer + merchant). Products: PayPal wallet, Venmo, Braintree (payments infra), PayPal Checkout, BNPL, and PayPal Open (commerce tools). Identity is critical infrastructure at PayPal — consumer sign-in, two-sided marketplace trust, fraud prevention, and merchant authentication. Notable 2026 signal: Joy Chik (President of Identity & Network Access at Microsoft) joined PayPal's Board in March 2026, signaling that identity is a board-level priority. ([PayPal SEC DEF 14A 2026](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1633917/000119312526145721/d59508ddef14a.htm))
