# GoFundMe

## Business Overview
GoFundMe is the world's largest crowdfunding platform for personal and nonprofit fundraising. It acquired Classy (GoFundMe Pro) to expand into institutional/nonprofit giving. The platform spans personal fundraisers, Giving Funds, Profiles, and Intelligent Ask Amounts. Identity is a core engineering investment — GoFundMe is building an identity platform covering consumer identity lifecycle, anonymous-to-authenticated transitions, visitor stitching, account linking, progressive trust, and confidence scoring. ([GoFundMe](https://www.gofundme.com); [Greenhouse JD](https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/gofundme/jobs/7680986))
