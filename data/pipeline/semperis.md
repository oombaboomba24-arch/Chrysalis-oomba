# Semperis

## Business Overview
Semperis is a cybersecurity company specializing in Active Directory (AD) and Entra ID security, detection, and recovery. Founded 2014, Hoboken NJ. Products include Directory Services Protector (DSP) — a Gartner-recognized ITDR solution — and Active Directory Forest Recovery (ADFR). In February 2026, Semperis acquired MightyID to expand beyond AD into Okta and Ping multi-cloud identity environments. FedRAMP authorization targeted for 2026. Total funding ~$368M including $200M+ Series C led by KKR. Estimated ARR ~$200M. ([Semperis](https://www.semperis.com); [BankInfoSecurity, 2026](https://www.bankinfosecurity.com/semperis-buys-mightyid-to-expand-identity-security-a-30679))
