# OpenLoop Health

## Business Overview
OpenLoop Health is a Des Moines, Iowa-based telehealth infrastructure company providing white-label virtual care solutions — including licensed physicians, prescription processing, and clinical operations — for consumer-facing telehealth platforms. It powers the backend for dozens of telehealth brands. In January 2026, the company experienced a significant data breach affecting 716,000 individuals (PHI exposed), which has elevated the urgency of identity and authentication improvements. ([OpenLoop Health](https://openloophealth.com/); [HIPAA Journal](https://www.hipaajournal.com/openloop-health-data-breach/))
