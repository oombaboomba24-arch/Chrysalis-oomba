# Okta

## Business Overview
Okta is the leading independent identity platform, with $2.6B+ ARR (FY2026). Products span Workforce Identity Cloud (WIC) for enterprise SSO, MFA, and governance, and Customer Identity Cloud (CIC, powered by Auth0) for consumer/developer CIAM. Okta's 2026 strategy centers on agentic identity — securing AI agents alongside humans — and is the only major independent IAM vendor (vs. Microsoft/Google/AWS). CEO Todd McKinnon: "The only independent and neutral identity platform to secure identities from humans to AI agents." FY2026 guidance: 11% revenue growth, 26% non-GAAP operating margin. ([Okta SEC 8-K FY2026](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0001660134/000166013426000050/okta-4302026_ex991.htm); [Seeking Alpha, 2026](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4527901-okta-outlines-11-percent-fy-26-revenue-growth-target-while-advancing-ai-identity-security))
