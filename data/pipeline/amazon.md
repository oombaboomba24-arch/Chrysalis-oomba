# Amazon (AWS)

## Business Overview
Amazon Web Services is the world's largest cloud platform (~$108B ARR). The Quick Identity, Governance, and Security team is focused on transforming how enterprises use Agentic AI with Amazon Quick — driving identity and governance strategy for millions of users accessing data across AWS and third-party applications. Key priorities include multi-region DR, IAM federation to Identity Center migration, and direct Just-in-Time IDP integration. ([Amazon Jobs](https://www.amazon.jobs/en/jobs/3207441/principal-product-manager-technical-quick-identity-governance-and-security); [AWS Identity Docs](https://aws.amazon.com/identity/))
