# Adobe

## Business Overview
Adobe is a $21B+ ARR enterprise software company best known for Creative Cloud (Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere) and its rapidly growing Digital Experience segment (Experience Platform, Marketo, Analytics). Identity underpins every Adobe product — from consumer Creative Cloud sign-in to enterprise B2B SSO via Adobe Identity Management Service (IMS). The Principal PM – Identity role sits in platform and influences activation, retention, and trust across all Adobe products. ([Adobe Careers, 2026](https://careers.adobe.com/us/en/job/R164735/Principal-Product-Manager-Identity))
