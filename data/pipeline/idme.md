# ID.me

## Business Overview
ID.me is a digital identity verification company that provides federally-accredited identity proofing and authentication for government agencies, financial services, and enterprises. Used by 157M+ people, with 80M verified to federal IAL2/AAL2 standards. Expanding in 2026 to Medicare.gov (CMS) and FINRA in addition to existing VA, IRS, and state unemployment agency integrations. Business model: SaaS verification fees per transaction and per enterprise seat. ([ID.me Government](https://www.id.me/government); [FedScoop, 2026](https://fedscoop.com/medicare-gov-deploy-id-me-beneficiary-verification/))
